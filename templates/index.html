About Us

In our website, we are going to perform binary classification of various news articles and news reports available online with the help of concepts related to AI and Machine Learning. We aim to give user the ability to classify the news as fake or real and also give ability to check the authenticity of the websites or web pages which are publishing the news articles.

Rich Dataset

Data is gathered from 244 websites. It is made up of approximately 44898 posts that were recorded over the course of 30 days. The true news data set is made of 23481 posts and the fake news data set is made of 21417.

Data Cleaning

Text data was preprocessed before being input into machine learning and deep learning models, by employing NLP methods such as stop word removal, tokenization, sentence segmentation, and punctuation removal.

High Accuracy

We used the combination of two ML algorithms i.e. we used stacked model of Logistic Regression and Decision Tree which give accuracy more than 99.60%.

Fake News Detection

Our new era where the mobile internet is ubiquitous, every person depends on various resources which are available online for news. Due to the increase in use of social media news can be shared rapidly among various number of users within a very short duration. The spread of fake news has bad and far-reaching results like the creation of biased opinions which can lead to confusion and misunderstanding among various groups. Moreover, people who are spreading fake news use appealing news headlines to generate revenue using advertisements via click-baits. So it is important to detect fake news and stop spreading it.
